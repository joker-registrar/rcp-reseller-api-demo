Billing handle:
Domain/AUTH-ID pairs:
The domain/AUTH-ID pairs should be
separated with one or more spaces,
tabs, comma or semicolon. Only one
pair per line!

Example:
test-domain1.com abc123
test-domain2.com cba321

OR

test-domain1.com,abc123
test-domain2.com,cba321

OR

test-domain1.com;abc123
test-domain2.com;cba321

etc.
